    • About

    A replacement roll holder for label making, when an original roll holder(blue) went missing at my dad's work I was tasked with creating a working replacement that did not require a metal strip to bite into the roll. Using my alphawise U30 and some PLA I was able to come up with this six-pointed design which has been working flawlessly.

    What I learned

    • Creating an object design suited for its material
    • Developing a strong part for lasting use
    • Prototyping to find the best design
